Our Rooms

We have two comfortable bedrooms, one with a double bed, and one with twin beds which can be placed together.

Both rooms are en-suite and boast spectacular views of Kilbroney Forest Park, Carlingford Lough and the Mourne and Cooley Mountains. We provide off-road parking and we a have secure storage area for mountain bikes. We are committed to ensuring that our guests enjoy their stay with us.
