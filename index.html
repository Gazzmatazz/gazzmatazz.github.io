The Sands B&B

Welcome

The Sands B&B is situated in exclusive Victoria Square beside the sea and just a five minute stroll from the heart of the picturesque village with its shops, bars, and cafes.

Rostrevor is famed for its alpine scenery and this is the ideal location for exploring the Mourne Mountains and learning more about the unique history and landscape of County Down. It is perfect for walkers, climbers, mountain bikers, golfers, anglers, photographers, artists or folks who simply want to relax in tranquil surroundings. Rostrevor Forest, with its world class mountain bike trails, is right on our doorstep.

About Us

The Sands B&B is owned and run by husband and wife Anne and John Keane. They opened their home to the public in 2011 and are now a multiple award winning Bed and Breakfast that has featured on ITV and RTE television.

Anne and John are musicians and historians with a wealth of knowledge of the area and would be delighted to advise and guide you during your stay.
